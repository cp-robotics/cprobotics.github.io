About

Our mission is to grow as people and the prevalence of robotics not only at our school but around Omaha through camps and outreach programs. This all while focusing on being exemplary models of kindness and sportsmanship to others. We are the only competing team in Omaha, NE. Also we have made great progress in our rookie year, winning the Rookie Inspire Award at the Kansas City Regionals.


FAQ

Who are you?

We are 6805 CyberJays of Creighton Preparatory School, an all male Jesuit College Preparatory School in Omaha Nebraska.

How much experience do you have?

We were established during the 2018 Competition season. While we have only competed for one year, our team is mostly comprised of members who have past experience with other forms of competitive robotics.

What makes you special?

We are the only competing team in Omaha, NE. Also we have made great progress in our rookie year, winning the Rookie Inspire Award at the Kansas City Regionals. These being a one many other reasons.

How would we reach out to you?

Anyone can come up to us at a tournament and also we can be reached at any time through other methods shown on the Contact Us page, accessible by the link below.

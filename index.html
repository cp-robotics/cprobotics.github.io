ABOUT

The 6805 CyberJays are a robotics team originated from Creighton Prepartory Schools. They have prieviously participated in the Greater Kansas City Regional and more to come. They were founded in 2018 at Creighton Prep with the captain William Randby. You can find more about the school at https://www.creightonprep.creighton.edu.

ABOUT

Our mission is to grow as people and the prevalence of robotics not only at our school but around Omaha through camps and outreach programs. This all while focusing on being exemplary models of kindness and sportsmanship to others. We are the only competing team in Omaha, NE. Also we have made great progress in our rookie year, winning the Rookie Inspire Award at the Kansas City Regionals.

PROJECTS


FINDining VISIT

FINDining is a revolutionary new app that solves the problem of not knowing where to eat and not being able to find new, local restaurants. FINDining's advanced restaurant recommendation algorithm uses your eating preferences and tastes in order to give you unique restaurant recommendations.


RGBSOURCEDOWNLOAD

RGB is a love2d-based bullet-hell, rouge-like game. where the goal is to collect 255 values of red, green, and blue. You can complete this task by firing bullets at enemies that are colored one of the three colors.


AutoSecSOURCEDOWNLOAD

This ubuntu script is a modular, bash program that secures various parts of the Ubuntu 14.04 Operating System. The programs is specifically being scripting towards the cyber security competition, CyberPatriots.

ABOUT

Ben Hall is an expert in Operating System security in Ubuntu and Debian. He participated in the 2016-2017 CyberPatriots cybersecurity competition, making it to Semi-Finals and placing 58th in the United States. When he isn't addicted to video games, he spends his time creating graphic design and designing websites. Also, he created bash scripts to automate the hardening of operating systems like Ubuntu 14.04. He is currently working on the website for the 2017-2018 I.T Innovation company, FINDining. You can reach him at swidexx@gmail.com.
